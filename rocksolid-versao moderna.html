About RockSolid

Forward-thinking construction leadership with a mission that goes beyond buildings

Founded by Alexandra Santini, RockSolid Construction & Consulting is a purpose-driven design-build firm focused on transforming the U.S. construction sector through advanced technologies, energy-conscious systems, and scalable housing solutions.

Headquartered at 1078 Summit Ave Suite 744, Jersey City, New Jersey 07307, the company is built around two urgent priorities: expanding affordable housing access and advancing more responsible, resilient construction practices for residential, commercial, and public-sector clients.

Affordable Housing Scalable development models designed to reduce costs and increase access.
Sustainable Systems Energy-efficient materials and low-carbon construction strategies.
Technology Integration 3D printing, BIM workflows, and modern delivery coordination.
Community Impact Projects shaped around resilience, equity, and long-term value.
